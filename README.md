# The AI First Hire Kit

**Your first AI employee ... a Chief of Staff ... and the Company Brain it works from.**

Part of **The Content to Clients Challenge** by Truly Authentic Marketing. *Powered by TAMI.*

This kit turns a blank AI agent into a trained **Chief of Staff**: you name it, it interviews you, learns your business in your own voice, and builds your **Company Brain** ... the memory every piece of content and every other AI employee will run from. Human-first, not generic AI slop.

## How to use it (3 steps)

1. **Open your AI employee** ... Hermes (free desktop app), or any agent you like (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.).
2. **Give it this repo.** Paste the repo link and say:
   > *"Read this repository, then onboard me. Follow START-HERE.md."*
3. **Answer its questions.** First you name it ... then it interviews you about your goals, where you are now, your offer, your customer, and your voice, researches your website, builds your Company Brain, and writes you one piece of content in your voice. You review, it fixes, your voice is locked in.

That's Day 1.

> **Tip:** Install **Wispr Flow** so you can *talk* to your AI employee instead of typing the answers ... way faster. (Challenge link gives you a free month.)

## What your Company Brain holds

Your AI employee writes your business into plain markdown pages ... your story, your **goals and current stage**, your ideal customer in their own words, your **named offer**, your voice (the anti-slop rules), your real proof, and your AI employee's **name**. Any agent can read and write it.

It's best **viewed in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md)** (free): open the `company-brain-template/` folder as a vault and you'll see your business as a connected map ... people, offers, customers, proof ... with a live graph view. It's yours: open, edit, or delete anything any time.
