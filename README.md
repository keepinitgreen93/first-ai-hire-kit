# The AI First Hire Kit

**Your first AI employee — a Chief of Staff — and the Company Brain it works from.**

Part of **The Content to Clients Challenge** by Truly Authentic Marketing. *Powered by TAMI.*

This kit turns a blank AI agent into a trained **Chief of Staff** that interviews you, learns your business in your own voice, and builds your **Company Brain** — the memory every piece of content and every other AI employee will run from. Human-first, not generic AI slop.

---

## What's in here

| File / folder | What it is |
|---|---|
| `START-HERE.md` | The first thing your agent reads. Tells it what to do. |
| `chief-of-staff.md` | Who your AI employee is — its role, standards, and the way it works. |
| `ONBOARDING.md` | The exact Day 1 flow your agent runs: interview you → build your Company Brain → write your first post. |
| `company-brain-template/` | A blank Company Brain. Your agent fills this in during onboarding. Open it in **Obsidian** to *see* your brain. |
| `.claude/skills/brain/` | The `/brain` skill — the full system your agent uses to build and grow the brain. |
| `CLAUDE.md` | Config so the kit "just works" if you run it in Claude Code. |

---

## How to use it (3 steps)

1. **Open your AI employee** — Hermes (free desktop app), or any agent you like (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.).
2. **Give it this repo.** Paste the repo link and say:
   > *"Read this repository, then onboard me. Follow START-HERE.md."*
3. **Answer its questions.** It interviews you, researches your website, builds your Company Brain, and writes you one piece of content in your voice. You review, it fixes, your voice is locked in.

That's Day 1. 🎉

> **Tip:** Install **Wispr Flow** so you can *talk* to your agent instead of typing the answers — way faster. (Challenge link gives you a free month.)

---

## See your brain

Your Company Brain is just markdown files, so any agent can read and write it. But it's best **viewed in [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md)** (free): open the `company-brain-template/` folder as a vault and you'll see your business as a connected map — people, offers, customers, proof — with a live graph view.
